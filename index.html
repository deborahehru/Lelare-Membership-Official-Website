About us

Legendary Ladies Recruit (LeLARe) is a registered non-profit social organization giving empowering young women in Sub-Saharan Africa through mentorship programmes, career advice, internship placements, and job opportunities in the formal sector for African women. The goal is to significantly reduce and in the long run, eliminate barriers restricting young African women from attaining career heights as their global contemporaries.

With our vast network of mentors, partners and resources, we set young women in motion to achieve their career aspirations, find and fulfil purpose, while supporting one another to replicate the success. Since inception in November 2019, LeLaRe has mentored over 240 young women, seeing them from post-graduate levels to career fulfilment, and extended its mentorship programmes to 500 other high-school girls.

Mission

Our mission is to provide a diverse platform that grooms young African women through mentorship and resourceful programmes to crush their day-to-day barriers.

Vision

Our vision is to see young African women liberated, get more deeply rooted and represented across high-end carrers and empower millions to champion a new wave of the African future female leaders revolution.
